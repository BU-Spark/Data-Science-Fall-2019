    03/31/2010 #6.1 ORDER (Cordy, J.): I am in receipt of correspondence from Assistant District Attorney Moore dated March 26, 2010 and counsel for the petitioner Attoryney Eisenberg dated March 29, 2010. The eorrespondence concerns the status of the underlying commitment proceeding in the Superior Court with respect to which I have reserved and reported a question of law. That proceeding has now been stayed by an order of a Superior Court judge. I have some memory of a discussion at the hearing on October 7, 2009 of concerns about not unduly delaying the underlying commitment proceeding. The record of that hearing apparently was not properly recorded and preserved. I do not have a memory of issuing a directive with respect to the commitment proceeding, however, and absent a record, I am not inclined to act on the Commonwealth's request which, as I understand it, would be used in an effort to lift the stay. As I understand the status of the reservation and report, the matter is on for hearing before the full court in September. The petitioner is currently in custody, and has consented to delay in the underlying proceeding. The Commonwealth has not identified andy prejudice suffered by it, at least in its filings in this Court.
