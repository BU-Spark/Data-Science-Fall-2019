    02/13/2018 #23 ORDER to stay proceedings. (Budd, J.)...This matter came before the Court, Budd, J., on the parties joint motion for reservation and report in the above-reference, three consolidated petitions filed by the petitioner Conservation Law Foundation pursuant to G.L. c. 164, § 69P and G.L. c. 25, § 5, appealing related final decisions by the respondent Energy Facilities Siting board (EFSB). The parties' joint motion for reservation and report also requested that the related case New England Power Generators Assoc. & other v. MA Dep't of Envtl. Protection & others, SJ-2018-045, be reserved and reported to the full court in sequence with the three consolidated petitions. On February 9, 2018, this Court reserved and reported only SJ-2018-045 for reasons set forth in the reservation and report. See Paper No. 26 in SJ-2018-045. Upon consideration, it is ORDERED that these above-referenced, three consolidated matters be, and hereby are, STAYED pending the full court's consideration of the reservation and report of the related SJ-2018-045 matter and until further order of this Court."
