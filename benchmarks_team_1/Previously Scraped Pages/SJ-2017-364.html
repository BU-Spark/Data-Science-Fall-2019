    10/26/2017 #10 Memorandum of Decision: ... "I have before me two motions filed by the petitioner. The first is a motion to stay the proceedings in the lower court until this appeal is decided. The second is a motion, pursuant to Appellate Procedure Rule 12 (b) and the petitioner's in forma pauperis status, to waive the requirement of attaching an appendix and, instead, to have the matter heard on the original record. Petitioner previously moved for a motion to stay the proceedings in the lower court until this appeal was decided, which the lower court denied on August 8, 2017. I see no reason to overturn the judges ruling that the underlying case may proceed while this appeal is pending. As to petitioner's motion to waive the requirement of attaching an appendix pursuant to Appellate Procedure Rule 12 (b), I will deny it without prejudice. Petitioner may review this request once he is before the single justice who will be assigned to the appeal. It is therefore ORDERED that the petitioner's motion to stay the proceedings in the lower court is DENIED, and that the petitioner's motion to waive the requirement of attaching an appendix pursuant to Appellate Procedure Rule 12 (b) is DENIED WITHOUT PREJUDICE." (Gants, C.J.)
