    04/13/2010 #4 ANNOUNCEMENT: The Justices are soliciting amicus briefs. The Commonwealth secured an indictment of "John Doe" in 2006 with a physical description and a DNA profile; in 2008 the defendant's DNA was matched to the DNA profile accompanying the indictment and the Commonwealth successfully moved to amend the indictment to name the defendant. At issue are: whether the 15-year statute of limitations set forth in G. L. c. 277, § 63, bars prosecution of the defendant for rape and other crimes that occurred in 1991; whether an indictment that identifies a defendant primarily by his DNA profile is sufficient. Argument is scheduled for Fall 2010. Amicus submissions are due no later than two weeks before the first day of the sitting in which the case is scheduled for argument.
