    03/06/2018 #6 ORDER: The defendant seeks to appeal from the February 1, 2017 sentence in New Bedford District Court No. 1333CR6673. On January 29, 2018, defendant filed a Notice of Appeal and Motion To Extend The Time For Filing Of Notice Of Appeal. Leave was granted by the lower court and the Notice of Appeal was filed on February 1, 2018. Notwithstanding the trial court's allowance of the defendant's motion on February 1, 2018, the trial court may only extend the time for filing the notice of appeal for a period not to exceed 30 days, see Mass. R. App. Proc. 4(c), and after that time, a single justice of this court, for good cause shown, may extend the time for filing for up to one year. See Mass. R. App. 14(b). The lower court appears to have been without jurisdiction to grant the requested relief. Accordingly, the defendant may seek leave from the single justice under docket no. 18-P-162 to deem the late filed notice of appeal timely, or to show cause, in writing, why jurisdiction over this appeal is proper, on or before March 16, 2018. *Notice/Attest.
