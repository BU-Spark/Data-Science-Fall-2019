    07/15/2010 #2 ANNOUNCEMENT: The Justices are soliciting amicus briefs. The issue presented is whether non-criminal possession of marijuana may serve as probable cause for a search or seizure under the Massachusetts Constitution: whether the odor of burnt marijuana justified an exit order to a passenger in a motor vehicle which resulted in an admission of possession of a small rock of cocaine. Argument is scheduled for Fall 2010. Amicus submissions are due no later than two weeks before the first day of the sitting in which the case is scheduled for argument.
