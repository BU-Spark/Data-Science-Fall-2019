    03/17/2017   ORDER: The plaintiff is attempting to appeal from the Probate and Family Court, Middlesex Division judgment of dismissal docketed on 5/31/16. The notice of appeal was docketed on 7/1/16. It appears that the notice of appeal may be untimely, having been filed more than thirty days after entry of the judgment or order appealed from. See Mass. R. App. Proc. 4(a). Accordingly, the plaintiff is ordered to show cause, in writing, why jurisdiction over this appeal is proper, or move for voluntary dismissal pursuant to Mass. R. App. Proc. 29(b), or file a motion for enlargement of time to file the notice of appeal pursuant to Mass. R. App. Proc. 14(b). The failure to take action by March 27, 2017, may lead to dismissal of the appeal for lack of prosecution. *Notice/Attest
