    08/16/2017   RE#12: Upon further review of the impounded appendix that was rejected for filing, it is unclear why pages 31 through 72 of the record appendix have been segregated and designated as impounded. Massachusetts Rules of Appellate Procedure 16(d), 16(m), 18(a) and 18(g) detail the proper scope and treatment of impounded appendix materials. Specifically, only those materials that are impounded are to be included in an impounded appendix and instances where a confidential victim's name is used may simply be redacted rather then designating the entire document as impounded. The motion is denied without prejudice to renewal, on or before August 23, 2017, detailing the reasons why pages 31 through 72 of the appendix should be deemed impounded. Alternatively, a replacement appendix may be filed on or before said date. *Notice.
