    03/31/2016   Notice and Invitation to Join Electronic Filing Pilot: The Appeals Court has launched an electronic filing pilot in civil, non-impounded cases subject to review by a three-judge panel, which includes the above-referenced case. The pilot includes the electronic filing and service of many types of motions and other documents through the Massachusetts Court System Odyssey File and Serve Site, as well as the electronic payment of entry fees. In May, the pilot will expand to include electronic filing and service of briefs and appendices in civil, non-impounded panel cases. Any Massachusetts attorney may register to participate in the electronic filing pilot. The Appeals Court Order Concerning Electronic Filing Pilot Project, and helpful training and guidance materials are located on the Appeals Court FAQ Guidance page, see http://www.mass.gov/courts/court-info/appealscourt/efiling-appeals-faq-gen.html The Appeals Court encourages all attorneys in pending civil appeals to register and participate in the e-filing pilot. You must create an account and register to submit or receive service of electronic filings.
