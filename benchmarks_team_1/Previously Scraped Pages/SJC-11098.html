In this appeal from the denial of declaratory relief, whether the judge correctly ruled that the obligations of a close corporation and its stockholders set forth in the articles of incorporation, with respect to the stockholders' right to dispose of their shares, are determined by contract law rather than by fiduciary principles. See Chokel v. Genzyme Corp., 449 Mass. 272 (2007); A.W. Chesterton, Inc. v. Chesterton, 128 F.3d 1 (1st Cir. 1997); Pointer v. Castellani, 455 Mass. 537 (2009). The case is tentatively scheduled for argument in May..
