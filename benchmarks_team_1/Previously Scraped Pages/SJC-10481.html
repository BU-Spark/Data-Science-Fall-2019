    12/11/2009 #11 RESCRIPT (Full Opinion): Because the recreational aspects of the employee's service as a ski club chaperone were subordinate to the work-related duties she performed, G.L. c. 152, s 1(7A), does not bar her from receiving workers' compensation for her injury. Accordingly, the board's decision to award the employee medical benefits pursuant to G.L. c. 152, s 30, is affirmed. (By the Court) Reasons as on file. Notice sent.
