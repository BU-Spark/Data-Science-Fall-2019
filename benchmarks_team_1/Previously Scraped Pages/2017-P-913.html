    08/28/2017   Memo re: 2017-J-363 "ORDER: The defendant in this summary process action filed a request for review of a summary process bond order through the procedures set forth in G. L. c. 239, s. 5 (f)-(h). Accordingly, the matter was so assembled and transmitted by the Housing Court's clerk's office. Upon review of the materials provided by the Housing Court, it appears that the order challenged by the defendant is not the type of post-judgment order governed by the procedures in section 5(f)-(h) of the summary process statute. Instead, the challenged order is the Housing Court judge's allowance of a motion to stay levy on the execution subject to the defendant paying for use and occupancy. Whether the defendant was entitled to greater relief was already considered by a single justice and denied in 17-J-347. Thus, the defendant's request for review is treated as a notice of appeal from the Housing Court order granting the conditional stay. That appeal is hereby consolidated with the defendant's pending appeal in 17-P-913 without the need for further assembly of the record. The Housing Court shall forthwith transmit updated docket sheets. The defendant's brief in the consolidated appeal remains due on or before 09/15/2017. (Vuono, J.)."
