    03/16/2017   Under consideration by Panel. (Agnes, J., Kinder, J., Shin, J.).
    04/05/2017 #14 ORDER: Appellant's pro se brief, which purports to appeal the dismissal of a wrongful termination claim, is comprised solely of factual assertions interspersed with pages of the record appendix in no apparent order. It fails to (1) state the precise relief sought as required by Mass.R.A.P. 16(a)(5), and (2) cite any legal authority or reasoned explanation of error as required by Mass.R.A.P. 16(a)(4). See Howe v. Tarvezian, 73 Mass. App. Ct. 10, 12 (2008) ("Contentions lacking legal authority or reasoned explanation fall short of [appellate] argument."). As both a legal and practical matter, appellant's submission provides an insufficient basis for us reasonably to consider her claims. Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed. (Agnes, Kinder, Shin, JJ.[1]). (*** Inadvertently not docketed on 03/29/2017 ***) *Notice. Footnotes: 1. The panelists are listed in order of seniority.
