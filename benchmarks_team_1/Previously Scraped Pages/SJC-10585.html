In this case involving the construction of gravel access roads across several streams protected under the Wetlands Protection Act to access the site of a proposed wind farm development, the issue presented is whether the Superior court judge correctly ruled that proposed open-bottom culverts under bridges traversing certain streams were subject to the general standard (310 CMR 10.03(1)(a)(3)) for work in a buffer zone, rather than the five-part performance standards (310 CMR 10.54(4)(a)) for work on a resource are known a s a "bank." Argument is tentatively scheduled for March 2010. Amicus submissions are due on or before February 16, 2010.
