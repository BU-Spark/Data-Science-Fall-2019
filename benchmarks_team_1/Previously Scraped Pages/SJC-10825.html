    02/16/2012 #15 DENIAL of petition for rehearing. The petition for rehearing filed by the Commonwealth having been considered, it is ordered that the petition be denied. The following changes to the portion of the text of the court's opinion captioned "Discussion" have been made: (1) the seventh, eighth, and ninth paragraphs have been deleted; (2) the first word in the tenth paragraph "Moreover" has been deleted; and (3) the words "mistaken and" in the first sentence of the eleventh paragraph have been deleted. Justice Spina has also changed his dissent as follows: (1) the eleventh paragraph beginning "The court" has been modified; (2) the twelfth paragraph now beginning "Here, the judge" has been modified; and (3) the twenty-first paragraph beginning "The court" has been modified. Footnote numbering and cross-references have been updated as a result of these changes.(By the Court) Notice to counsel.
