    09/05/2017 #1 Appeal entered pursuant to M.G.L.c. 239, § 5 with attachments.
    09/11/2017   ORDER: A review of the lower court docket indicates that judgment for possession entered in favor of the plaintiff on 4/24/2017, and no timely notice of appeal was filed from that judgment. The plaintiff moved to levy on the execution on 7/31/2017, after which, on 8/2/2017, the defendants moved to stay the levy on the execution. On 8/7/2017, the lower court denied the defendants' motion to stay, and on that same date the defendants filed a notice of appeal from "the judgment ...entered on August 7, 2017." Also on 8/7/2017, the defendants filed 17-J-351, seeking a stay of the execution. It was noted in the order of the single justice that the filing was devoid of record materials to enable comprehensive review of the motion. In addition, on 8/7/2017, the defendants moved for relief from judgment in the lower court. The motion was denied by the lower court, after a hearing, on 8/14/2017, and their motion to set or waive the appeal bond was taken under advisement on that date. The defendants' motion for waiver of the summary process appeal bond was under review in the lower court at the time the single justice entered her order in 17-J-351 denying the motion for stay without prejudice to renewal subsequent to the completion of the statutory appeal bond review process. On 8/18/2017, the lower court's findings and order on the appeal bond were entered on the docket. On 8/21/2017, the defendants filed a request for review of the appeal bond order with the single justice. On 9/1/2017, the lower court transmitted the notice of assembly of the record for review of the appeal bond order to this court. The only notice of appeal filed by the defendants appears to be from the 8/7/2017 order denying their motion to stay the levy on the execution. As no timely notice of appeal from the judgment for possession was filed, no automatic stay is in place, and the appeal bond provisions of G.L. c. 239, s. 5 do not apply. See G.L. c. 239, ss. 3 & 5. Accordingly, the request for review of the appeal bond pursuant to G.L. c. 239, s. 5 is hereby dismissed. (Lemire, J.). *Notice/Attest/Chaplin, J.
