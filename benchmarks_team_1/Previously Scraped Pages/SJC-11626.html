    10/07/2014 #8 ORDER: This matter is pending before us pursuant to our Order Establishing a Modified Procedure for Appeals in Bar Discipline Cases, Mass. Ann. Laws Court Rules, Standing Orders of the Supreme Judicial Court, at 713-714 (LexisNexis 2013). In an order dated May 2, 2014, we remanded the matter to the Board of Bar Overseers so that the board could indicate expressly whether it viewed the respondent's conduct as negligent or intentional and to explain the basis for its recommendation. The board has since reported to the court that it unanimously found the respondent's misuse of funds to be intentional, and has filed an explanatory memorandum. We now remand this matter to the single justice for any reconsideration he may care to give the matter in light of the board's July 14, 2014, vote and its memorandum dated July 16, 2014. The clerk of the full court shall provide copies of this order, our order of May 2, 2014, and the board's July, 2014, vote and memorandum to the clerk of the county court for the single justice's consideration. Further proceedings in this appeal shall be stayed pending consideration by the single justice. By the Court.
    02/27/2015 #9 ORDER: The stay of proceedings ordered on October 7, 2014, is hereby vacated. Bar counsel shall indicate within ten days any reason she believes this appeal should not be dismissed in light of the single justice's order of indefinite suspension dated February 11, 2015, and, absent any reason, shall file either a stipulation of dismissal or a motion to dismiss pursuant to Mass. R. A. P. 29 (b). If and when the respondent appeals from the single justice's order of indefinite suspension, the clerk shall enter that appeal as a new appeal, with a new number, on the court's docket. By the Court
    03/10/2015 #10 STIPULATION OF DISMISSAL without fees or costs. (PER THE WITHIN, PURSUANT TO MASS. R. A. P. 29 (b), THE APPEAL IS DISMISSED.) Notice sent.
