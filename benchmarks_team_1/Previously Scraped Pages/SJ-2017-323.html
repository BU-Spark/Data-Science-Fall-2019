    10/05/2017 #9 ORDER: ... "on a petition seeking twofold relief concerning the petitioner's pretrial detention in the above-referenced New Bedford District Court matter. The petition seeks a writ of habeas corpus and declaratory and injunctive relief. In response to the petition, the respondents filed a motion to dismiss the petition in its entirety. Upon consideration, and after hearing on this matter, it is ORDERED that the respondents' motion to dismiss be, and hereby is, allowed in part and denied in part. The respondents' request to dismiss the portion of the petition seeking a writ of habeas corpus is allowed on the grounds of mootness. The respondents' request to dismiss the portion of the petition seeking declaratory and injunctive relief, however, is denied on the grounds set forth in the petitioner's opposition. Accordingly, it is FURTHER ORDERED that the respondents shall file their answer to the complaint for declaratory and injunctive relief (including counts I-V), on or before October 20, 2017. Because the complaint raises significant legal issues capable of repetition and likely to evade review, the Court is considering whether to reserve and report the complaint for declaratory and injunctive relief to the full Court. Accordingly, it is FURTHER ORDERED that the parties shall jointly file a statement of agreed facts on or before November 3, 2017. In order to assist the Court in consideration of this matter, the statement of agreed facts shall be as comprehensive as possible, and shall include, at a minimum, the various facts that were represented to me by counsel at the two hearings on this matter concerning both this petitioner and the intentions of the respondents going forward with respect to other detainees in similar circumstances." (Budd, J.)
