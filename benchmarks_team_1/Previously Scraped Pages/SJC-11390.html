    03/07/2013 #4 ANNOUNCEMENT: The Justices are soliciting amicus briefs. The issue presented is whether a trustee may be found to have breached its fiduciary duties to the income beneficiary of a trust by failing to follow the investment recommendations of an investment advisor, particularly where the trustee invests in fixed income funds and instruments . See the Uniform Prudent Investor Act, G. L. c. 203C. The case is scheduled for Fall 2013.
