    02/28/2013 #7 ANNOUNCEMENT: The Justices are soliciting amicus briefs. In this Land Court case involving a vacant lot of registered land, with no frontage on a public way but accessible by rights of way within the subdivision described "as shown" on a specific Land Court plan, the issue presented is whether, in a claim by the easement holder that his full use of the travel easement was obstructed, changes could be made to the location or dimensions of the easement so long as the easement's utility was not lessened, or whether all encroachments on the right of way must be removed. See Restatement (Third) of Property (Servitudes) § 1.2 (2000); M.P.M Builders, LLC v. Dwyer, 442 Mass. 87 (2004). Argument is scheduled for Fall 2013..
