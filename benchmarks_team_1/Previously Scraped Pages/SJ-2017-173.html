    05/02/2017 #4 ORDER to stay proceedings. (Cypher, J.) "This matter came before the Court, Cypher, J., on an emergency petition pursuant to G.L. c. 211, § 3, seeking review of the May 1, 2017 order of the Superior Court, Fahey, J., denying petitioner's motion to recuse herself from the trial. Petitioner seeks a stay of the trial scheduled to begin on May 3, 2017. Upon consideration, it is Ordered that petitioner's motion for stay be, and the same hereby is, Allowed, and the proceedings in Middlesex Superior No. 1581CR0084 are stayed pending further order of the Court. It is FURTHER ORDERED that a hearing on the motion to recuse will be held on Wednesday, May 10, 2017, the scheduling of the hearing allows the Court time to adequately prepare, as well as to allow the parties time to obtain and prepare whatever written material and pleadings they choose to file. Any such pleadings shall be filed no later than May 8, 2017."

    05/05/2017 #7 ORDER: "This matter came before the Court, Cypher, J., on a G.L. c. 211, §3 petition of an order of the Middlesex Superior Court dated May 1, 2017 and docketed May 3, 2017 in docket number 1581CR0084. On May 1, 2017 the petitioner filed an emergency motion to stay the trial court proceeding. That motion was allowed on May 1, 2017. (Cypher, J.). On May 5, 2017 the petitioner filed a notice withdrawing the emergency petition for relief pursuant to G.L. c. 211, §3 seeking leave from an order denying recusal. Upon consideration, it is hereby ORDERED that the stay of the trial court proceeding entered on May 1, 2017, is VACATED. Further, the notice withdrawing the emergency petition for relief pursuant to G.L. c. 211, §3 seeking leave to appeal from an order denying recusal, is ALLOWED, without hearing."
