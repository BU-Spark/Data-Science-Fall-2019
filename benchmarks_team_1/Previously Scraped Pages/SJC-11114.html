    12/23/2011 #2 The Justices invite interested individuals and organizations to submit briefs addressing the request for an advisory opinion that was transmitted by the Governor to the Justices on December 22, 2011, relative to the Executive Council. Specifically, the Governor has asked the Justices: "Is it constitutionally required that the governor be physically present at and presiding over an assembly of the Executive Council in order for the Lieutenant Governor to vote on matters before the Council, provided that the chair of the Governor is not vacant by reason of the Governor's death, absence from the Commonwealth, or other incapacity?" Briefs must be filed on or before Friday January 13, 2012. Notice sent.
