    07/30/2008 #3 ANNOUNCEMENT: The Justices are soliciting amicus briefs. On appeal from a conviction for possession of marijuana with intent to distribute involving about half an ounce of marijuana in fiftenn small plastic bags, the issue presented is whether the judge correctly allowed a police officer to give an expert opinion that the marijuana evidence was "more consistent with distribution" than with personal use, based on assertions that had no apparent empirical foundation.
