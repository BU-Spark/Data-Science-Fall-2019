    02/03/2017   ORDER: On December 16, 2016, the petitioner in the underlying probate matter, Merrilee J. Kerr-Ford, filed a notice of appeal from the denial of her motion for leave to file amended petition for informal appointment of personal representative. A review of the Probate and Family Court's docket indicates that an appearance and objection was filed on December 30, 2016 and that the further proceedings on the petition are anticipated in the Probate and Family Court. Thus, it appears that the petitioner may have impermissibly appealed from an interlocutory order. The petitioner is ordered to (1) move for voluntary dismissal of the above-captioned appeal pursuant to M.R.A.P. 29 (b); or (2) to show cause, in writing, why jurisdiction over this appeal is proper on or before February 17, 2017. *Notice/Attest
