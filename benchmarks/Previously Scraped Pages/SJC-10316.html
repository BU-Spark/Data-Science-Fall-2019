    12/29/2008 #12 ANNOUNCEMENT: The Justices are soliciting amicus briefs. The issue presented, among others, is whether a special permit granted in 1985 had lapsed pursuant to to G. L. c. 40A, s. 9, and Bellingham Zoning By-Law s. 1550, in circumstances in which the first two of the four approved phases were begun in 1986 and completed in 1987, but the request for a modification of the special permit and development plan approval in connection with the construction of the third and fourth phases was not submitted to the planning board until 2006; whether construction of the first two phases constituted commencement of a substantial use of the permit such as would avoid lapse. The case is tentatively scheduled for February 2009 argument.
