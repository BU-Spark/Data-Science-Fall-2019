    07/17/2012 #19 ORDER: The appellants' motion for costs is denied. An award of costs to the appellants is not equitable here where the appellants did not prevail on all issues raised, where the judgment of the trial court was simply vacated and not reversed, and where the decision as to whether and where the access road will be built, and it dimensions, still rests with the trial judge and the Truro planning board. See Mass. Rule App. 26 (a). By the Court. Notice to counsel.
