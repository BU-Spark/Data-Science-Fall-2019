    06/06/2017   ORDER: The defendant, 331 Lafayette, LLC, appealed from an order denying its special motion to dismiss pursuant to G. L. c. 184, s. 15. The order denying the special motion to dismiss was entered on the Superior Court docket on December 5, 2016. Pursuant to G. L. c. 231, s. 118 (second para.) and Mass. R. A. P. 4(a), the notice of appeal was due within 30 days of the date of entry of the order. The notice of appeal appears, according to the Superior Court's docket, to have been filed on January 6, 2017. Therefore, it appears the notice of appeal was late. See G. L. c. 231, s. 118 (second para.) and M.R.A.P. Rule 4(a). The defendant is ordered to move for voluntary dismissal of the above-captioned appeal pursuant to M.R.A.P. 29 (b), or to show cause, in writing, why jurisdiction over this appeal is proper on or before June 20, 2017. *Notice/Attest
