    05/22/2015 #16 RESCRIPT (Full Opinion): For the reasons set forth herein, we conclude that Article 5 of NECS's articles of incorporation contained a valid agreement to arbitrate future controversies regarding the value of NECS's stock, but that no such controversy existed at the time of NECS's motion to compel arbitration. Therefore, for reasons other than those set forth by the motion judge, the order denying NECS's motion to compel arbitration is affirmed. (By the Court)
