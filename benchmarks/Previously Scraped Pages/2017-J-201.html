    09/06/2017 #7 MEMORANDUM AND ORDER: The stay of appellate proceedings is vacated. I have reviewed the petition to determine whether to grant the defendants leave to appeal the Land Court judge's order to a full panel of this court . This case does not present the extraordinary circumstances warranting such relief. See Long v. Wickett, 50 Mass. App. Ct. 380, 387-389 (2000) (discussing "the appellate courts' traditional abhorrence of piecemeal appellate review"). Thus, the petition is denied. (Lemire, J.). *Notice/Attest/Krupp, J.
