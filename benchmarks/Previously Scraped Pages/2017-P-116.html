    02/01/2017 #3 Docketing Statement received from Shawn A. McGonagle
    02/02/2017   ORDER: On March 31, 2016, the defendant was convicted of assault and battery in violation of G. L. c. 265 § 13A(a) and sentenced the same day. According to the Boston Municipal Court - Roxbury Division's docket sheets, the defendant filed his pro se notice of appeal on May 24, 2016. "In a criminal case . . . the notice of appeal required by Rule 3 shall be filed with the clerk of the lower court within thirty days after entry of judgment or order appealed from; or entry of a notice of appeal by the Commonwealth; or the imposition of sentence." Mass. R.App. P. 4(b). Consequently, it appears the defendant's notice of appeal is untimely. Further, the defendant filed a motion for leave to file a late notice of appeal with a single justice of this Court on July 19, 2016 (16-J-297). Due to his failure to comply with a procedural order, the entry of the motion to docket late on the single justice docket was vacated without a ruling on the motion. Therefore, the plaintiff is ordered to (1) move for voluntary dismissal of the above-captioned appeal pursuant to M.R.A.P. 29 (b); (2)to re-file a motion for leave to file a late notice of appeal under docket number 17-P-116; or (3) to show cause, in writing, why jurisdiction over this appeal is proper on or before February 16, 2017. *Notice/Attest
