    12/29/2014 #2 ANNOUNCEMENT: The Justices are soliciting amicus briefs. Whether a Superior Court judge exceeded his authority by imposing sanctions on an attorney for one of the plaintiffs in these multiple consolidated actions, in excess of $200,000, on the ground that he had interfered with an emerging global settlement of the actions by contacting other potential plaintiffs, notifying them of the impending settlement, and inviting them to contact him for representation of their like claims after the settlement was finalized; whether, as the attorney contends, the judge erred in assessing sanctions without first conducting an evidentiary hearing and, allegedly, without adherence to any existing standard.
