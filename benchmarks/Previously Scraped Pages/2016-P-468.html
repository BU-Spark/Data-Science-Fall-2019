    04/15/2016   ORDER: After pleading guilty to the charges, Defendant is attempting to appeal from the Hampden Superior Court's (Kinder, J.) pre-trial order denying his motion to suppress. Pursuant to Mass. R. Crim. P. 15 (a) (2), such an appeal must be taken prior to trial. See also Commonwealth v. Zion, 359 Mass. 559, 562-563 (1971) ("By pleading guilty the defendant foreclosed his right to request a decision by this court on legal questions which he had raised prior to entering the guilty pleas."). Defendant is ordered to move for voluntary dismissal of the above-captioned appeal pursuant to M.R.A.P. 29 (b), or to show cause, in writing, why jurisdiction over this appeal is proper on or before April 29, 2016. (Agnes, J.). *Notice/Attest.
