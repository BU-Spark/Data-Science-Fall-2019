    04/14/2010 #2 ANNOUNCEMENT: The Justices are soliciting amicus briefs. In an action to recover for personal injury and property damage arising from the presence of chronic damp and mold in an apartment, in which the judge found two of the three defendants had willfully and knowingly violated G. L. c. 93A, the issue presented is whether the judge properly awarded punitive (double) damages jointly and severally rather than independently and severally against those two defendants. Argument is scheduled for Fall 2010. Amicus submissions are due no later than two weeks before the first day of the sitting in which the case is scheduled for argument.
