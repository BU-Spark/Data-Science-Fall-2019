    02/17/2012 #6 ANNOUNCEMENT: The Justices are soliciting amicus briefs. The issue presented, among others, is whether the Superior Court judge correctly affirmed the decision of the Housing Appeals Committee overturning a decision of the town's zoning board of appeals that had denied a comprehensive permit under G. L. c. 40B, for the construction of 136 apartment units on the grounds that: the plan created a parcel that was noncompliant with zoning requirements of access ("infectious invalidity"); the plan contravened the town's master plan by unilaterally expanding the sewer district; and lack of regional need. Paired with Sunderland Zoning Board of Appeals v. Sugarbush Meadow, LLC & another, SJC-11100. The case is tentatively scheduled for argument in May.
