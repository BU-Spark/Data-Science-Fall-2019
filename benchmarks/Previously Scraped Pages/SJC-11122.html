This action challenges the constitutionality of St. 2008, c. 524 (An Act Further Regulating the Rights of Adopted Children), effective July 1, 2010, as applicable to pre-1958 trust instruments; whether the testatrix's intention to exclude adopted issue controls her dispositions pursuant to G. L. c. 191, § 1A(2); whether application of the Act is fundamentally unfair where a subsequent generation undertook irrevocable estate planning in reliance on prior law. The case is tentatively scheduled for argument in May.
