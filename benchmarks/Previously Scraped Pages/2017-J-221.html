    07/10/2017   RE#13+ #14: The motion for stay of the February 23, 2017 contempt judgment ( paper #13) and the motion to enlarge time for filing a notice of appeal from that judgment ( paper # 14) are denied. Contrary to this court's June 26, 2017 order, the motions fail to "either (a) set forth why the notice of appeal assertedly filed after May 3, 2017 was timely...or (b) show...good cause for the late filing..." This court will postpone, for seven calendar days from the date of this order, any action on the motion to stay the February 1, 2017 order ( paper # 12) and the motion to enlarge time for filing a notice of appeal from that order ( paper # 15). Within that time the petitioner may obtain from the trial court clerk's office and file, as specified in this court's June 26, 2017 order, a "complete and updated trial court docket sheet reflecting that a notice of appeal, listing the February 1, 2017 order as the subject of the appeal, has been filed." The docket sheet filed with papers # 12 and #15 failed to reflect the filing of any such notice of appeal. Failure to file such a docket sheet within the seven calendar days will result in the denial of those motions. (Sacks, J.). *Notice
