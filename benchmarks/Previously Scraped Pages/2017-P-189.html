    03/03/2017   ORDER: On or about December 1, 2016, the plaintiffs filed a notice of appeal from the judgment in Suffolk Superior Court docket number 1484CV01836. However, judgment did not enter on that docket until December 12, 2016. Thus, it appears that the notice of appeal was filed prematurely. On or before 3/16/17, the appellants shall (1) move for voluntary dismissal of the above-captioned appeal pursuant to Mass. R.App. P. 29 (b); (2) show cause, in writing, why jurisdiction over this appeal is proper notwithstanding the apparently premature notice of appeal; or (3) move for leave to file a late notice of appeal from the judgment. Further, it is noted that only Edwin Parris has docketed his appeal pursuant to MRAP 10. The other plaintiffs may seek to late docket their appeals by motion. *Notice/Attest
