    03/27/2014 #3 CERTIFIED QUESTIONS 1. Does the employee misclassification test under Mass. Gen. Laws ch. 149, s.148B, determine the employee status of a worker seeking to recover from an employer the cost of workers' compensation insurance premiums under the Wage Act. 2. Does the Wage Act prohibit a contractual arrangement in which an employee agrees to have sums deducted from his earnings for employer-supplied accouterments and equipment that are necessary for the employee's performance of the job, specifically in this case mandatory uniforms and package scanners for package pick-up and delivery drivers?. 3. Does the Wage Act prohibit a contractual arrangement in which an employee agrees to pay a third party (without reimbursement) for expenses such as vehicle maintenance and operation, that are necessary for the performance of the job? SO ORDERED. Richard G. Stearns UNITED STATES DISTRICT JUDGE
