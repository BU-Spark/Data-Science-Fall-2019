    04/11/2011 #14 RESCRIPT (Full Opinion): The order allowing the plaintiff's motion for summary judgment, and the declaration entered in the plaintiff's favor, are vacated. The order denying the defendant's motion for summary judgment is reversed. A new judgment shall enter declaring that the regulations codified at 314 Code Mass. Regs. ss 4.05 (3)(b)(2)(d), 4.05 (3)(c)(2)(d), 4.05 (4)(a)(2)(d), 4.05 (4)(b)(2)(d), and 4.05 (4)(c)(2)(d) (2006) are a valid exercise of the authority vested in the Department of Environmental Protection by the Clean Waters Act, G.L. c. 21, s 26-53.
