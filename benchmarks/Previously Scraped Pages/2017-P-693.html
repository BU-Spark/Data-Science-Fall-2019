    06/06/2017   ORDER: The defendant appeals from her convictions in, inter alia, Lawrence District Court docket number 1318CR001826, 1318CR001883,1318CR002668 and 1318CR002919. However, the convictions associated with those docket numbers were placed on file, suspending both the judgment and the defendant's right to appeal the conviction. See Commonwealth v. Delgado, 367 Mass. 432, 438 (1975) (Absent exceptional circumstances, appellate courts do not consider claims of error until judgment has entered). Therefore, the defendant is ordered to move for voluntary dismissal of the above-referenced appeals pursuant to M.R.A.P. 29 (b), or to show cause, in writing, why jurisdiction over those appeals is proper on or before June 20, 2017. This order does not pertain to the convictions for which the defendant was sentenced in the remaining Lawrence District Court cases appealed herein. *Notice/Attest
