    01/06/2017   ORDER: A summary process judgment entered for the plaintiff and against the defendant on November 15, 2016, and on November 23, 2016, the defendant timely filed a notice of appeal from the judgment and a motion to waive the appeal bond. On December 21, 2016, the trial court issued an order denying defendant's motion to waive the appeal bond after finding she was not indigent nor did she have a non-frivolous appellate issue. On December 29, 2016, within the period of time for which she could seek review from the single justice of the appeal bond order, the defendant filed a motion for reconsideration of the order in the trial court. The trial court denied the motion for reconsideration on January 5, 2017, but revised the appeal bond order to state that the defendant was indigent. She now seeks review of the appeal bond order and order on the motion for reconsideration. Defendant's request for review is hereby transmitted to the trial court. The trial court is to assemble the record for purposes of single justice review of the appeal bond order pursuant to G.L. c. 239, s. 5 (f) and (g). Upon assembly of the record, it is to be transmitted to this court for automatic entry under the statute, after which a hearing will be scheduled. Accordingly, the order entered in the trial court on January 5, 2017 is hereby stayed pending entry and disposition of the appeal bond review. (Sullivan, J.). Notice/Attest
    01/06/2017   RE#1: ALLOWED FORTHWITH. The petitioner's Affidavit of Indigency appears regular and complete on its face, and indicates that the petitioner is indigent. The court hereby allows the Motion to Waive entry fee. Notice Sent.
    02/01/2017   RE#2: As this matter was reassembled in the trial court and entered on the single justice docket as 17-J-16, this case is closed. Refer to 17-J-16 for proceedings related to review of the appeal bond order. *Notice/attest/Winik, J.
