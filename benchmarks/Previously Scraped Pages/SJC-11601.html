    01/21/2015 #7 RESCRIPT (Full Opinion): Because we hold that the motion for required findings of not guilty was improperly denied, we do not consider the defendant's double jeopardy claims. We reverse the denial of the motion for required findings of not guilty as to the charges of murder and assault with intent to murder, and we direct entry of verdicts of not guilty as to those indictments. The case is remanded to the Superior Court for sentencing on the indictments alleging illegal possession of firearms and accessory after the fact to murder. (By the Court)
