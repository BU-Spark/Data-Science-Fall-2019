    08/02/2012 #6 ORDER: Appeal allowed to proceed. Brian Michael Olmstead appeals from a judgment of a single justice of this court dismissing his appeal from a decision of the Department of Telecommunications and Cable pursuant to G. L. c. 25, § 5. He has filed a "Memo on Appeal or Application to Obtain Further Review," and appears to be attempting to comply with S.J.C. Rule 2:21, as amended, 434 Mass. 1301 (2001). That rule applies only where an interlocutory ruling of the trial court is at issue; it does not apply here. Accordingly, Olmstead may proceed with his appeal in the regular course (and has already received leave from this court to extend the date by when he must file his brief to October 15, 2012). Notice sent.
