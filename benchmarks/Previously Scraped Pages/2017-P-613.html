    05/11/2017   ORDER: The plaintiff Annie Griffin appeals from an order docketed by the by the Boston Housing Court on April 20, 2016, which concludes "the motion for judgment on the pleadings is allowed and termination of the subsidy is permitted as set forth herein. Judgment shall enter for the BHA." Thereafter, the next docket entry is Plaintiff's notice of appeal on May 16, 2016. A review of the subsequent docket entries to date does not reflect the entry of final judgment on the Housing Court's docket as contemplated in the Housing Court's April 20, 2016 order. It appears that this Court may not have jurisdiction to review this appeal because the notice of appeal may have been premature, having been filed before final judgment entered on the trial court docket. See Mass. R. Civ. P. 54 and 58(a); Mass. R. App. P. 4(a); G.L. c. 231, § 113. Accordingly, on or before May 26, 2017, the plaintiff is ordered to move for voluntary dismissal of the above-captioned appeal pursuant to M.R.A.P. 29 (b), or show cause, in writing, why jurisdiction over this appeal is proper. The failure to take action may lead to the dismissal of this appeal for lack of prosecution. *Notice/Attest
