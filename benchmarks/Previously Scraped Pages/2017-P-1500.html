    11/30/2017   ORDER: The defendant seeks to appeal his conviction and sentencing on March 20, 2017. On October 12, 2017, the defendant filed a motion to file a late notice of appeal in the Superior Court, which was allowed on October 13, 2017 ( Rufo, J.) The notice of appeal was then filed on October 13, 2017. Notwithstanding the Superior Court's allowance of the defendant's motion on October 13, 2017, the lower court may only extend the time for filing the notice of appeal for a period not to exceed 30 days, see Mass. R. App. Proc. 4(c), and after that time, a single justice of the Appeals Court, for good cause shown, may extend the time for filing up to one year. See Mass. R. App. Proc. 14(b). Accordingly, the defendant is ordered to show cause, in writing, why jurisdiction over this appeal is proper, or file a motion for voluntary dismissal pursuant to Mass. R. App. Proc. 29(b), or file a motion for enlargement of time to file a notice of appeal pursuant to Mass. R. App. Proc. 14(b) under this docket number. The failure to take action by December 14, 2017, may lead to dismissal of this appeal for lack of prosecution. *Notice/Attest/Rufo, J.
